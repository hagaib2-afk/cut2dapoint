Cornermate Table

Precision corner cutting integrated into your production line.

Overview

The Cornermate is purpose-built for corner operations. Swap blade cartridges to cut radiused or notched corners — without recalibration. The integrated positioning table guarantees repeatable placement every cut, every time.

Popular with sign shops running high-mix jobs and fabricators doing edge finishing on chromaLuxe, aluminum, and composite panels. The Cornermate Table reduces hand finishing time and delivers the clean geometry your customers expect.

The Cornermate combines precision engineering with straightforward operation. Every lever, blade, and frame has been refined over years of shop feedback.

  • Precision XY positioning table with mechanical stops
  • Quick-change blade cartridge system
  • Compatible with Round Corner, Notch, and Chamfer blades
  • Optical cross-hair positioning for alignment
  • Manual clamping or optional pneumatic hold-down
  • Built-in swarf tray with extraction port
