Overview

The Cornermate is purpose-built for corner operations. Swap blade cartridges to cut radiused or notched corners — without recalibration. The integrated positioning table guarantees repeatable placement every cut, every time.

Popular with sign shops running high-mix jobs and fabricators doing edge finishing on chromaLuxe, aluminum, and composite panels. The Cornermate Table reduces hand finishing time and delivers the clean geometry your customers expect.

The Corner Cutter Press (RA10051) is a compact benchtop tool designed for precision corner operations. Swap between 6 interchangeable die profiles—no recalibration needed—making it ideal for shops that run multiple corner profiles on the same job.

  • System Model: RA10051 with 6 die profiles (RA10052, RA10087, RA10053, RA10054, RA10088, RA10089)
  • Quick-change interchangeable die system
  • Compact footprint (150×150mm) — saves bench space
  • Hardened tool steel dies for durability
  • Manual lever operation with spring return
  • Transparent acrylic guide plate for visibility
  • Powder-coated steel base for stability
  • Built-in waste collection chamber

Materials Compatibility

The Corner Cutter Press works with:

  • Plastic sheets and acrylic (up to ~30mm stack capacity)
  • Paper and cardstock (multiple sheets)
  • Leather and composite materials
  • Thin decorative metals
  • ChromaLuxe and metal-composite panels
Die Compatibility

All 6 dies are designed to work interchangeably with the RA10051 press — no special tools or adapters needed. Each die slides into the cutting head holder and is secured with a single M4 grub screw.

Available die profiles: Round (1/4" and 1/8"), Notch, Chamfer (45°), Curvy S-curve, and Scallop.
